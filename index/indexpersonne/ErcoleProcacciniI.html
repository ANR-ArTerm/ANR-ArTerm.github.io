Ercole I Procaccini

peintre italien
- Date de naissance : 1520
- Lieu de naissance : Bologna
- Date de mort : 1595-01-01
- Lieu de mort : Milano

Rôle : Painter, he studied with Prospero Fontana and the two artists worked together in Rome in 1551. Active in Parma before 1560 and during part of that decade, in 1569 Ercole is documented back in Bologna. Ercole was active as an officer in the newly formed painters’ guild Compagnia dei Pittori between 1569 and 1585 and its head from January to April 1585, just before he left Bologna. The Procaccini family moved to Milan in the mid-1580s. Comment on works: Religious dit Ercole Procaccini il Vecchio ou Ercole Procacino
