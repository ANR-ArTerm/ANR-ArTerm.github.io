Reni Guido

peintre italien (1575–1642)
- Date de naissance : 1575-11-04
- Lieu de naissance : Bologna
- Date de mort : 1642-08-18
- Lieu de mort : Bologna

Rôle : Reni created easel paintings and large decorations in Rome, Naples, Mantua, and Bologna, for patrons including Pope Paul V and Italy's top royalty. His graceful, classical style featured refined colors, delicate and varied flesh tones, soft modeling, and gentle emotion that owes a debt to Raphael's work.
