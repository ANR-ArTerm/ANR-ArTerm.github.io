peintre et graveur brabançon (1525-1569)
- Date de naissance : 1525
- Lieu de naissance : Breda
- Date de mort : 1569-09-09
- Lieu de mort : Bruxelles

Rôle : Possibly born in Breda and likely trained by Pieter Coecke van Aelst. Bruegel is considered one of the greatest artists in 16th-century northern Europe. His work, mainly landscapes, scenes of peasant life and allegory, is a blend of religion, folklore, and humanism. The influence of his work was widespread and long-lasting.
