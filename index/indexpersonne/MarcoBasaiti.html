Marco Basaiti

peintre italien
- Date de naissance : 1470-01-01
- Lieu de naissance : Venise
- Date de mort : 1530-01-01
- Lieu de mort : Venezia

Rôle : Recent scholarship has corrected Vasari's assertion that Marco Basarini and Marco Basaiti were separate people. The artist signed his work variously. It has been stated that he was of Albanian or Greek origin, and also that he was born in Friuli: none has been conclusively proven. Comment on works: religious, Portraits Marc Basarini chez Monier
