Battista Dossi

peintre italien
- Date de naissance : 1490-01-01
- Lieu de naissance : San Giovanni del Dosso
- Date de mort : 1548-01-01
- Lieu de mort : Ferrare

Rôle : Battista's work is often overshadowed by that of his older brother, Dosso, with whom he often collaborated. He spent nearly his entire career in service of the Ferrara court, generally working for his apparently much older brother. From 1517 he may have worked in Rome in Raphael's workshop. A "Nativity" in Modena is often regarded as a work illustrating Battista's own style, the classical references and clear style reflecting his early experience with Raphael. A number of works are also documented by Battista after Dosso's death in 1542, including the "Justice" and the "Hour Leading forth the Horses of Apollo" in Dresden. After his brother's death, Battista thrived as the head of a busy workshop involved in various court commissions. Comment on works: religious ou Battista de Luteri
