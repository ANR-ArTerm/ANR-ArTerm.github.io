Giovanni Francesco Barbieri

peintre italien
- Date de naissance : 1591-02-08
- Lieu de naissance : Cento
- Date de mort : 1666-12-22
- Lieu de mort : Bologna

Rôle : Son of a laborer who transported wood. He was called Guercino (squinter), because he was cross-eyed or had a squint in his right eye. Reputedly self-taught early, then an apprentice in the shop of Benedetto Gennari. Patrons included the papal legate at Ferrara and the Archbishop of Bologna. He developed a classical style later in his career. After the death of Guido Reni, Guercino was the leading artist in Bologna. He is also known as a brilliant draftsman.
