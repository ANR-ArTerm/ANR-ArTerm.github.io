François Du Quesnoy

sculpteur flamand
- Date de naissance : 1597-01-12
- Lieu de naissance : Bruxelles
- Date de mort : 1643-07-12
- Lieu de mort : Livorno

Rôle : Leading figure in circles devoted to classical art; friend of Poussin; major works are the statues of Sta Susanna (Sta Maria di Loreto) and St Andrew (St Peter's); famous for his putti and small bronzes; assisted Bernini on the Baldacchino in St Peter's. Comment on works: Sculptor
