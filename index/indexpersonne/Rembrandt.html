Rembrandt, van Rijn

peintre et graveur néerlandais (1606–1669)
- Date de naissance : 1606-07-15
- Lieu de naissance : Leiden
- Date de mort : 1669-10-04
- Lieu de mort : Amsterdam

Rôle : Noted as one of the most popular and influential artists of his period. His work is characterized by the Baroque interest in dramatic scenes and strong contrasts of light on a dark stage. The subjects of his works include paintings, etchings, and drawings of subjects including portraits, landscapes, figures, animals, biblical and secular history and mythology.
