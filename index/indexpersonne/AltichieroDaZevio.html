Altichiero da Zevio

peintre italien
- Date de naissance : 1330-01-01
- Lieu de naissance : Zevio
- Date de mort : 1390-01-01
- Lieu de mort : Vérone

Rôle : Noted as one of the most influential north Italian artists of the Trecento. His work referred to the humanist culture of Petrarch and his associates in the courts of Verona and Padua. His known career began after 1364, when he painted the Sala Grande of the Scaligeri Palace in Verona. Italian painter, Verona. Comment on works: religious
