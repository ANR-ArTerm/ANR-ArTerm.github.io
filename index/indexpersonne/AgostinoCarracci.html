Agostino Carracci

peintre et graveur italien
- Date de naissance : 1557-08-16
- Lieu de naissance : Bologna
- Date de mort : 1602-03-22
- Lieu de mort : Parma

Rôle : Agostino left his profession as a tailor to become an artist. His earliest works were engravings, which was an extremely profitable practice and he was famed for having attained new heights of perfection. He was also a skilled painter and collaborated with his famous relations on various commisions. Comment on works: Religious; Portraits.
