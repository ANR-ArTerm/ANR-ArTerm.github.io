Luca della Robbia

sculpteur italien (1399-1482)
- Date de naissance : 1399-07-01
- Lieu de naissance : Florence
- Date de mort : 1482-02-20
- Lieu de mort : Firenze

Rôle : Son of Simone di Marco della Robbia, a member of the Arte della Lana, the wool-workers’ guild in Florence. Noted as a pioneer of Florentine Renaissance style; was the founder of a family studio primarily associated with the production of works in enameled terra-cotta. Comment on works: Sculptor, ceramist
