Francesco Vanni

peintre italien
- Date de naissance : 1563-01-01
- Lieu de naissance : Siena
- Date de mort : 1610-10-26
- Lieu de mort : Siena

Rôle : Francesco Vanni's mother, Battista Morelli, daughter of a Sienese silversmith and widow of Eugenio Vanni, married Arcangiolo Salembeni on 20 April 1567. Francesco studied with his stepfather alongside Ventura Salembeni, his stepbrother. After Arcangiolo's death in 1580, he went to Bologna and then Rome, where he entered the workshop of Giovanni de Becchi. He then conducted most of his career in Siena. Comment on works: Religious; Landscapes.
