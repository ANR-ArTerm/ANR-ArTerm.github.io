Donatello

sculpteur italien
- Date de naissance : 1386-01-01
- Lieu de naissance : Firenze
- Date de mort : 1466-12-13
- Lieu de mort : Florence

Rôle : Important and very influential Florentine sculptor in the media of marble, bronze, and wood. Although he himself was not not a cultured intellectual (as was typical for artists of his generation and earlier), he associated with early Renaissance humanists in Florence, Rome, and Padua. In his art, he made new references to Antique forms, and to this added new expressive and technical virtuousity to sculpture of the period. Italian sculptor. Comment on works: sculptor dit Donatello
