Agostino Musi Veneziano

graveur italien
- Date de naissance : 1490
- Lieu de naissance : Venezia
- Date de mort : 1540
- Lieu de mort : Roma

Rôle : Musi was a pupil of Giulio Campagnola; he formed his style on a study of Albrecht Dürer and was also a follower of Marc Antoine Raimondi. He is noted for having published engravings of grostesque ornament similar to that by Raphael and Giovanni da Udine in the Vatican Logge. He probably died in Rome. dit Agostino Veneziano
