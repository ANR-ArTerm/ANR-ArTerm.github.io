Nicolas Langlois

artist, publishers, engraver
- Date de naissance : 1640-01-01
- Lieu de naissance : Paris
- Date de mort : 1703-01-01
- Lieu de mort : Paris

Rôle : Son of the publisher François Langlois. After his father's death, he was legally precluded from entering into partnership with his stepfather, so he set up his own business, 'La Victoire,' in Paris. Like his father, a part of whose stock he recovered, he published many almanacs and illustrated books on architecture, geometry, and mathematics. He also published prints by artists such as Paul Androuet Du Cerceau, Jean Boulanger, Jean Berain the elder, and Jean Le Pautre. He was succeeded in his business by his son, Nicolas Langlois the younger (1670-1707). Imprimeur
