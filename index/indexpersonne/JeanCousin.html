peintre, sculpteur et graveur français
- Date de naissance : 1490
- Lieu de naissance : Sens
- Date de mort : 1560
- Lieu de mort : Paris

Rôle : Possibly born in Souci, near Sens. Very little documented work by this successful artist survives; exceptions are three tapestries from a series on the life of St. Mammès, the painting "Eva Prima Pandora," and two windows in Sens Cathedral. In 1560 he published a treatise on perspective. He probably died in Paris. Comment on works: Portraits; History
