peintre italien
- Date de naissance : 1517-01-01
- Lieu de naissance : Pomarance
- Date de mort : 1597-01-01
- Lieu de mort : Città della Pieve

Rôle : His style was influenced by Florentine and Roman Mannerism. He is particularly noted for fresco cycles in S. Apollinare and S. Stefano Rotondo, Rome, following the didactic style influenced by post-Tridentine mandates, including emotional portayals of the tortures of Christian martyrs. His later works show a transition from Mannerism to a more monumental style. His last identified work is dated 1596. dit Le Pomarancio
