Cristoforo Foppa

sculpteur, graveur et orfèvre italien (1452-1527)
- Date de naissance : 1452
- Lieu de naissance : Italia
- Date de mort : 1527-01-01
- Lieu de mort : Rome

Rôle : Son of Maffeo Foppa, goldsmith, and Fiora de Carminalibus de Brambilla. Place of birth based on Caradosso's signatures "Caradosso del Mundo" and "Caradosso de Mondo". According to Giorgio Vasari, a 16th-century biographer of artists, Caradosso designed the medal which was placed under one of the crossing piers of Saint Peter's during a ceremony on 18 April 1506. The medal is one of the few sources for the design of Bramante's St. Peter's project. dit il Caradosso
