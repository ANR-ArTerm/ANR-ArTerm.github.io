Rogier van der Weyden

peintre primitif flamand
- Date de naissance : 1390
- Lieu de naissance : Tournai
- Date de mort : 1464-06-18
- Lieu de mort : Bruxelles

Rôle : Van der Weyden was a popular and influential artist. He apparently learned much from Robert Campin (the Master of Flémalle). He was a talented draftsman and designer, emphasizing with the emotional impact of his work and meticulous technical execution. He seems to have run a large and busy workshop, which complicates the attribution of works to him or his assistants. Netherlandish painter. Comment on works: Religious aussi Roger de Le Pasture ou Roger de Bruges
