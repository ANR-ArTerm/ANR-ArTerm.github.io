peintre et sculpteur italien
- Date de naissance : 1509-01-01
- Lieu de naissance : Volterra
- Date de mort : 1566-04-14
- Lieu de mort : Roma

Rôle : Highly talented and individual follower of Michelangelo; influential works include his fresco of the "Deposition" and a bust of Michelangelo made from his death mask. Ironically, he is famous for painting draperies over nudes in Michelangelo's "Last Judgement." Italian painter, muralist. Comment on works: History connu sous le nom de Daniele da Volterra, Daniello da Volterra ou il Braghettone
