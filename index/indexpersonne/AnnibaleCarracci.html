Annibale Carracci

peintre italien
- Date de naissance : 1560-11-03
- Lieu de naissance : Bologna
- Date de mort : 1609-07-15
- Lieu de mort : Roma

Rôle : Annibale was considered one of the greatest Italian painters of his age. His style is marked by naturalism combined with the formal language of Classicism in a grand and monumental style. Annibale was also instrumental in evolving the ideal, classical landscape and is generally credited with the invention of caricature. Italian painter. Comment on works: Mythology.
