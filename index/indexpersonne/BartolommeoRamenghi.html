Bartolomeo Ramenghi

peintre italien
- Date de naissance : 1484-01-01
- Lieu de naissance : Bagnacavallo
- Date de mort : 1542-01-01
- Lieu de mort : Bologna

Rôle : He was probably working in the workshop of Francesco Francia in Bologna, ca. 1503. Raphael was the dominant influence on his later works; the claim the Ramenghi assisted Raphael on the decoration of the Vatican Logge is unsubstantiated. Comment on works: Religious
