Cristoforo Roncalli

peintre italien
- Date de naissance :
- Lieu de naissance : Pomarancio
- Date de mort :
- Lieu de mort : Roma

Rôle : As a young man, he worked in Florence and Siena. He later established a career in Rome, first collaborating with Niccolò Circignani, who was also called Il Pomarancio (from the name of their home town) on a fresco cycle depicting the Discovery and Exaltation of the True Cross connu sous le nome de "Pomarancio".
