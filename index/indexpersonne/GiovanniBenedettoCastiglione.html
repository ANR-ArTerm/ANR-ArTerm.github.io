Giovanni Benedetto Castiglione

peintre et graveur italien
- Date de naissance : 1609-03
- Lieu de naissance : Genova
- Date de mort : 1665-01-01
- Lieu de mort : Mantova

Rôle : Versatile and eclectic artist open to foreign influence which was unusual for an Italian artist of his time. Some of his best-known works are somewhat fantastical. He was court painter at Mantua from 1648. Comment on works: Religious; Landscapes; Animals; Mythology.
