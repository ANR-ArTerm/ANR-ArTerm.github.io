Giulio Cesare Procaccini

peintre et graveur italien
- Date de naissance : 1574
- Lieu de naissance : Bologna
- Date de mort : 1625-11-14
- Lieu de mort : Milano

Rôle : Painter and sculptor, son of Ercole Procaccini. Having moved to Milan with the rest of the family in the mid-1580s, he trained as a sculptor, perhaps in the workshop of Francesco Brambilla (ii), and then worked (1591–9) for the workshop of Milan Cathedral. Italian painter, printmaker.
