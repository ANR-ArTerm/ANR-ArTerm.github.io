Frans I Floris

peintre flamand
- Date de naissance : 1519
- Lieu de naissance : Antwerpen
- Date de mort : 1570-10-01
- Lieu de mort : Antwerpen

Rôle : He was a leading exponet of Romanism in Antwerp, and he is largely responsible for introducing the Italian system of studios organized with multiple assistants to the Netherlands. Michelangelo's 'Last Judgment,' which he saw being unveiled, made an indelible impression on him and his religious and his mythological works are typically crowded with Mannerist nudes.
