- Date de naissance : 1510-01-01
- Lieu de naissance : Firenze
- Date de mort : 1563-11-11
- Lieu de mort : Roma

Rôle : Francesco de' Rossi was called "Il Salviati" because Cardinal Giovanni Salviati was an early patron. Son of a velvet weaver, Francesco was at first apprenticed to a goldsmith then to painters. He was a leading member of the generation of Florentine painters after Pontormo and Rosso Fiorentino; known for his elegant, complex High Mannerist style.
