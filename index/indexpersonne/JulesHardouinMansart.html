Jules Hardouin-Mansart

architecte français
- Date de naissance : 1646-04-16
- Lieu de naissance : Paris
- Date de mort : 1708-05-11
- Lieu de mort : Marly

Rôle : Student and great-nephew of François Mansart (1598-1666), architect. He directed the construction and design of Versailles from 1678 and designed the Dôme des Invalides from 1679 to 1691, which was added to the existing church begun in 1670, Paris. Ennobled in 1683, and became the Baron de Jouy, and then the Comte de Sagonne in 1693. Attained the post of "Superintendant des bâtiments du roi" in 1699.
