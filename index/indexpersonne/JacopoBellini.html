Jacopo Bellini

peintre vénitien du XVe siècle
- Date de naissance : 1396-01-01
- Lieu de naissance : Venezia
- Date de mort : 1470-01-01
- Lieu de mort : Venezia

Rôle : Italian painter, born in Venice likely in 1396 and died in that same city. Jacopo was an influential artist in his time, although most of his paintings are now lost. His surviving works are primarily small-scale, intimate devotional paintings and around 300 drawings (in two volumes, Paris, Louvre and London, British Museum). The drawings are unique among surviving 15th-century drawings for one artist, in terms both of the number and scope of the works. Jacopo's large-scale picture cycles and most important commissioned works have been destroyed, and are known only through documentary evidence and contemporary sources. Italian painter, Venice. Comment on works: Portraits, religious
