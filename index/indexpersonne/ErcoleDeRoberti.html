Ercole de' Roberti

peintre italien
- Date de naissance : 1450-01-01
- Lieu de naissance : Ferrara
- Date de mort : 1496-01-01
- Lieu de mort : Ferrare

Rôle : Scholarship in the past suggested that there were two painters working in Ferrara at this time, with the first name of Ercole. One had been given the name 'de' Roberti', the younger was designated 'Grandi'. However, it is now clear that they refer to the same artist. Comment on works: Portraits; Religious Ercole Ferrarese Laurent Hercule de Ferrare pour Monier ?
