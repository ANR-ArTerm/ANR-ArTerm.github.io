architecte est sculpteur italien
- Date de naissance : 1475
- Lieu de naissance : Milano
- Date de mort : 1555-09
- Lieu de mort : Milano

Rôle : Sculptor who worked on plans for the north portal of the Milan Cathedral. He executed a relief intended for the door which depicted the Presentation of the Virgin. This is now situated on the left pier at the entrance of the Lady Chapel. Lombardi also supervised and advised on other important religious buildings in Milan. dit le Lombardino
