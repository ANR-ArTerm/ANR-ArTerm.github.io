peintre et graveur italien maniériste (1535-1612)
- Date de naissance : 1535
- Lieu de naissance : Urbino
- Date de mort : 1612-09-30
- Lieu de mort : Urbino

Rôle : Italian miniaturist and painter who specialized in alterpieces. He worked in the Vatican for Pope Pius IV, where an attempt was made to murder him by poison. In injured health as a result, he returned to Urbino and died there. Comment on works: Religious dit Fiori da Urbino ou Baroccio (le Baroche en français). On trouve aussi Federico Barozzi chez Lomazzo.
