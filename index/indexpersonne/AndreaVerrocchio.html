Andrea del Verrocchio

sculpteur, peintre et orfèvre italien de l'école florentine, XVe s.
- Date de naissance : 1435
- Lieu de naissance : Firenze
- Date de mort : 1488
- Lieu de mort : Venezia

Rôle : Verrocchio was active as a sculptor and painter in Florence in the second half of the 15th century. He had a workshop, in which Leonardo da Vinci trained, and a wide range of patrons, including the Medici family, the Venetian State and the city council of Pistoia.
