Jacopo Barbari, de'

painter, printmaker and miniaturist
- Date de naissance :
- Lieu de naissance : Venezia
- Date de mort : 1516
- Lieu de mort :

Rôle : He was born about 1450 and may have been in contact with Dürer when the German artist first visited Venice in 1494. In 1500 Barbari was working in Dürer's home town, Nuremberg, as painter to the emperor. Barbari subsequently worked at other German courts and ultimately in the Netherlands, in the service of Mary of Austria
