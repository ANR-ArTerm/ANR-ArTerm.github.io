peintre et dessinateur néerlandais (c. 1450-1516)
- Date de naissance : 1450
- Lieu de naissance : Bois-le-Duc
- Date de mort : 1516-08
- Lieu de mort : Bois-le-Duc

Rôle : Considered the most distinctive and idiosyncratic of 15th-century Netherlandish artists, he produced a body of work remarkable for its depiction of fantastic, often diabolic, creatures, generally moralizing representations of the consequences of sin and folly. He was the first European artist to give free rein to almost uncontrolled association, formal inventivity, and free choice of ideas. Comment on works: Allegory dit Jérôme Bosch, ou Jheronimus Bosch
