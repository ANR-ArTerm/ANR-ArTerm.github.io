Agnolo Bronzino

peintre italien
- Date de naissance : 1503-11-17
- Lieu de naissance : Monticelli
- Date de mort : 1572-11-23
- Lieu de mort : Firenze

Rôle : Painter whose style dominated Florentine painting from 1530s-1560s. He was court artist to Cosimo I de’ Medici. Known for sophisticated style and extraordinary technical ability. Pontormo’s Mannerist style was the major formative influence on Bronzino’s art. Comment on works: History dit le Bronzino Francois Bronzin chez Monier ?
