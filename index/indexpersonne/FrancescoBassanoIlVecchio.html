Francesco I Bassano

peintre italien
- Date de naissance : 1475
- Lieu de naissance : Vicenza
- Date de mort : 1539-01-01
- Lieu de mort : Bassano del Grappa

Rôle : Painter in the Veneto. He was born in Vicenza, son of a tanner; after training in Venice he established himself as a painter at Bassano. Despite the war between Venice and the League of Cambrai (1508-1518), Francesco's workshop was successful during this time. He served on the town council of Bassano del Grappa, in the Veneto. His sons and several other descendants were also artists. Comment on works: Religious connu sous le nom de Francesco Bassano il Vecchio, l'Ancien en français
